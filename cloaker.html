Description

The NebulaGamez website offers a unique feature in the form of an about:blank tab cloaker. This feature allows users to create a disguised version of any specified website by creating an about:blank tab with a custom title and favicon, enabling discreet browsing without revealing the actual website being visited. With the ability to choose from a selection of presets or create a custom title and favicon, users can easily tailor their cloaked tabs to their liking. This can be particularly helpful for students who want to browse the internet without their teachers knowing which websites they are visiting. With the about:blank tab cloaker, you have complete control over your privacy and can browse the web with peace of mind.

How to use

  1. Enter the URL of the website you want to cloak in the "Enter link to website" field.
  2. Click the "Find Favicon" button to open the Favicon Finder.
  3. When the Favicon Finder appears, click on the desired favicon to copy its link to your clipboard.
  4. Paste the favicon link into the "Enter link for icon" field.
  5. Enter a custom title for the cloaked tab in the "Enter title for tab" field.
  6. Click the "Cloak" button to create the about:blank tab with the specified title and favicon.
  7. Enjoy discreet browsing!
